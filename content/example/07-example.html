More Needs Assessment

If you are interested in needs assessments or simply want more information, consider downloading the text A Guide to Assessing Needs written by Ryan Watkins, Maurya West Meiers and Yusra Laila Visser for the World Bank.

The authors provide a very detailed breakdown of needs assessments and how to conduct varying types of analyses. While in-depth quantitative or qualitative analyses of your needs assessment is likely the scope of this course, future highly structured approaches may warrant these methods. The text really does a good job of breaking what to do where and when.
