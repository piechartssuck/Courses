Note about the posts

Remember this is a graduate level class and your writing should reflect that. Moreover, these postings should be in APA 7th Edition formatting unless otherwise noted. The following general idea will be used when assessing your initial posts, but this is not a rubric.

  • ✔+: (11.5 points in gradebook) Posts are well defined responses that fully address and develop all aspects of the prompt such that they
    • advance the discussion,
    • are factually correct,
    • are cited correctly when necessary, and
    • provide a clear, concise, reflective and substantive contribution.
  • ✔: (10 points in gradebook) Posts well defined responses that mostly address all aspects of the prompt such that they
    • generally advances the discussion,
    • are factually correct,
    • are cited correctly when necessary, but
    • lack clarity or full development of the concept(s).
  • ✔−: (5 points in gradebook) Posts responses that address some aspects of the prompt where responses are
    • not clear or
    • ill-prepared or
    • repetitive or
    • superficial or
    • unsubstantiated.
  • ✘: (0 points in gradebook) Posts responses that are
    • non existent or
    • incorrect or
    • irrelevant to the discussion or
    • off-topic.

Otherwise 0 points (0%) in gradebook.
