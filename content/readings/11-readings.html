Post

  1. Read and write a single post under #mixed-methods that compares, contrasts, and synthesizes:
    1. Greene, Caracelli, & Graham (1989),
    2. Hanson (2005), and
    3. Idler (1999) Then discuss which you found to be the most interesting and why. If you did not find any to be of interest, please state your reason.

  2. Read Shenton (2004) and write a single post under #trustworthiness that address the following
    • if you identify mostly or fully with quantitative methodological approaches:
      1. State this in your title somewhere.
      2. Discuss your agreement or disagreement of Shenton’s argument. Please be thorough and justify.
      3. Ask a question about the reading that you believe a qualitative person should be able to answer.
    • if you identify mostly or fully with qualitative methodological approaches:
      1. State this in your title somewhere.
      2. Discuss your agreement or disagreement of Shenton’s argument. Please be thorough and justify.
      3. Ask a question about the reading that you believe a quantitative person should be able to answer.
