Pilot Study Ledger

In this class, you will plan, design, pilot, distribute, analyze and report on a survey from start to finish. After receiving a thumbs up from your planning task, in this second step you will create a draft survey that will eventually be administered in one form or another to your approved population.

Process

You may begin the pilot process as soon as you read this. Please be aware that the minimum standard for a pilot study are two iterations.

Reduction or Exemption

In rare circumstances, a reduction or in exemption from the iterations for the pilot are granted. If you believe that a sufficient case exists for either, please schedule an appointment to be taken place no later than October 7 by 5:00 PM.

Submit

There is one submission for this task: A csv or excel document outlining the history of your changes and submitted to eCampus by October 11 by 11:59 PM. You must account for every item within your survey in the order they appear in your online variant. This includes, but is not limited to the landing and gratitude pages. Please use the documents below to keep an accurate record as you will be using it to create a skeleton of your survey.

  • pilot_ledger.csv
  • pilot_ledger_codebook.csv
