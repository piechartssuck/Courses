Final Survey and Skeleton

In this class, you will plan, design, pilot, distribute, analyze and report on a survey from start to finish.

Process

  1. Take this week to set up your survey for distribution and to check it thoroughly. If you would like me to look at it, simply send a request over Slack or email and I will be happy to do so.
  2. Pick a day and time for distribution.
  3. Check the Survey Options panel.
  4. Choose a distribution method.
  5. Set up your communications which consist of notifications (if applicable), solicitations and gratitude In the meantime, you will also receive feedback on your pilot ledger. Once approved, you may go ahead and administer it1.
  6. (Optional) Set up a meeting if you would like assistance for a final check.

:::

  1. This process does not apply for those who have been granted an exception typically due to some external academic factor.↩︎
