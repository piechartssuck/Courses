Plan of Analysis

In this class, you will plan, design, pilot, distribute, analyze and report on a survey from start to finish. The material below is highly recommended, especially for those who do not have a great deal of experience administering surveys as a primary investigator (PI).

Process

  1. Take this week to think and finalize a plan for analysis. Remember that different types if questions require different types of analyses If you would like to discuss, simply send a request over Slack or email and I will be happy to meet.
  2. Keep a detailed record of the types of analyses. These will be important in your report and to some degree, your presentation as well.

Please note you do not have to turn this in as a separate task. :::
