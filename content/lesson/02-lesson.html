Data Camp

This week please complete both the Introduction to Data in R and Exploratory Data Analysis in R courses on Data Camp. The modules cover the:

  • Language of data
  • Study types and cautionary tales
  • Sampling strategies and experimental design
  • Case study (physical appearance of instructors and course evals)
    and
  • Exploring Categorical Data
  • Exploring Numerical Data
  • Numerical Summaries
  • Case Study (case study of email spam)

respectively.

A Note About the Title

That 80% figure is based on the Pareto Principal and is meant to be taken as a hyperbole. It roughly translates to the idea that 80 percent you time is spent simply finding, cleansing, and organizing data - aka wrangling - leaving only 20 percent for meaningful analyses.
