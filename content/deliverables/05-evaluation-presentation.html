Evaluation Presentation

Submission

eCampus

Type

Teams1

Description

Construct this as if you were going to present it to your primary stakeholder/sponsor.

Criteria

This submission is due one week prior to the last day of class2 and should be written in a format that is (1) professional yet accessible by your audience and (2) aesthetically pleasing. Please note that this does not need to be in APA formatting.

Structure

There is no specific formal outline to this deliverable, though you presentation must adhere to the following conditions AND follows the general components listed above in order:

  • Administered in an accessible format (e.g. HTML, PowerPoint, xaringan, YouTube, etc);

  • Narrated with detailed audio on each slide with equal group participation throughout;

  • Incorporates at least two (2) figures from the report must be included that strongly support your narrative;

  • Includes at least two (2) thoughtful prompts predicated on your study towards the end of the talk;

  • Provides a total running length of 10 minutes (+/- one minute); and

  • Submitted as a single presentation.

:::

  1. Students in the course must participate in the writing process in a group oriented setting.↩︎

  2. Please check eCampus for the exact due date.↩︎
